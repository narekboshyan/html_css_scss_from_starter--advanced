Question 1

Which of the following rgb colors results in the following shade of green:

(don't use a color picker! Just look at the color codes and work it out!)

1
2
3
4

Correct Answer

4

rgb(0, 200, 0)

Explanation

Correct! This color has lots of green in it (the 2nd channel) and very little red/blue.

Question 2

Which of the following hex color codes is equivalent to rgb(255,255,255) ?

1
2
3

Correct Answer

3

#FFFFFF

Explanation

Correct! #FFFFFF is "maxed out" red, green, and blue which gives us WHITE!
